Federal Directorate of Education Islamabad

Instructions

READ IT CAREFULLY

1. Carefully verify each student's data. Editing or deleting data will not be possible under any circumstances after submission to FDE.
2. Generate a Challan using the button provided on this page. (FDE and PEIRA Institutes).
3. After generating the Challan, download the consolidated list of students using the button available in the left navigation menu on the homepage and print it.
4. Pay the fee at NBP Bank. This step is applicable only for PEIRA and private students.
5. Verification of the paid Challan is required through the Treasury Office.
6. Submit the Challan copy along with the consolidated list of students to FDE.
